All Nintendo Switch games

This is a list of all Nintendo Switch games that I’ve played, in approximate order of preference. This is just my opinion, which is highly subjective and is based purely on how I’m feeling today. I plan to revise the list regularly; titles may go up as well as down.

Games in italics are awaiting review. They are strong candidates for moving in the list when that review happens.

  1. Breath of the Wild
  2. Super Mario Maker 2 — since SMM2 came out, I’ve pretty much been obsessed by it. It’s a fantastically good game, with almost endless playability, and might even come close to toppling the almighty BoTW, eventually.
  3. Super Mario Odyssey
  4. Hollow Knight
  5. Celeste — post-review, Celeste gets a bump up to 5th place in my overall listing. It got better and better as the game progressed, and there’s definitely lots of post-game content to go back to.

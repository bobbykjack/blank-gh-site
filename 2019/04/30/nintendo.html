Nintendo

I’ve loved Nintendo games for many decades; the affair began with the Mario Bros. Game & Watch system, which was released in 1983 (I probably got hold of it a couple of years later). I’ve owned about half the main consoles Nintendo has released since.

I’m currently working through the task of reviewing the 20 Switch games I own, including The Flame in the Flood, SteamWorld Dig 2, and Dig Dog.
